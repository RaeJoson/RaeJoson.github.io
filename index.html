Name

Hello! I am Rae Frances A. Joson, but you can call me Rae. R is the first letter of my name because it is also the first letter of my father’s name. My second name starts with the letter F because my mother’s name starts with the letter F. Rae means ewe or female sheep. They chose sheep because of many reasons. Sheep are usually very quiet, gentle, and obedient animals. It is among the most popular animals beloved by mankind. Sheep are extremely intelligent animals capable of problem-solving. Sheep is a valuable animal. In Jesus' day, sheep were prized possessions and you were a wealthy man if you owned large flocks. God compares us to sheep in the bible because to Him, we are priceless. My second name, “Frances” is from Our Lady of Peñafrancia.My parents named me after the Our Lady of Peñafrancia because I was conceived in September. It is the month when we have activities to show our devotion to Our Lady of Peñafrancia because it is the month of her feast day.
